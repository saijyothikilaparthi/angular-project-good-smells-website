FLORAL ATTARS

Made from the essence of flowers like jasmine, rose, or lavender. Light, fresh, and sweet, perfect for everyday wear or aromatherapy.

WOODY ATTARS

Features deep, earthy, and grounding fragrances derived from woods like sandalwood, cedarwood, or agarwood (oudh), Often used for its calming and meditative properties.

EXOTIC ATTARS

Combines rare and luxurious ingredients like amber, musk, or exotic spices. Known for its unique and rich aroma, ideal for special occasions.
